ABOUT US

А15 — is a team of attorneys with more than 10 years of bar practice, who defend freedom of speech and win over the censorship. Our name is Article 15 of the Constitution, which prohibits censorship in our country. Our name reflects our efforts in development of the free and diverse Ukrainian society, built on freedom of speech and pluralism of thoughts. We believe that this is impossible without guaranteed security and privacy as well in real life, and online.

Our Law Firm concentrates on legal representation in cases related to the freedom of speech and combating censorship, digital security and privacy, while Charitable Fund with the same name founded by the Law Firm provides free legal counselling and assistance for journalists, bloggers, activists and all those, whose voices are not heard, and who became victims of censorship.

WE DO NOT TRACK YOU
